- **Generative AI:** A type of AI technology that can create new content such as text, images, etc., resembling human-created content.
- **Supervised learning:** A machine learning method that requires labeled training data to teach the model to make predictions or decisions.
- **Neurons:** Nerve cells that are the basic building blocks of the nervous system. In the context of AI, they are often used as a metaphor for the basic processing units in artificial neural networks.
